Rules and Info (For low ranks):

  • PD - Police Department

    have 3 floors (F0, F1, F2)

    F0 is garage, arsenal room, hospital..

    F1 is jail room, waiting room for interview, kitchen..

    F2 is mission room, meeting room, offices..

  • Basic RP - RolePlay

    If you die, you must wait 15 minutes; after that, you can go back to action.

    Don't use a taser for long distances, and don't use it against deadly weapons.

    Drive safely to avoid VDM, It is also called DB (DriveBy). When you cross someone, stop, say sorry, and make sure the person is okay.

    For example, when it's You vs. +3 Criminals, try to find a safe place and call backup in radio. Try not to be part of the fights until you get a higher rank.

  • Green Zone at HP - Hospital

    Inside the hospital you can't arrest. (Inside the hospital is a safe place to chill out with other players.)

    Outside and near the hospital is the Green zone. When you see someone breaking RP rules, like Car killing (VDM or DB), collect evidence in photo or, better yet, video form and report it to Admins.

    Don't arrest borne criminals outside HP; give them some time to breath.

  • Army Base

    You cannot go to an Army Base without a good reason and permission from a rank 10 or higher. You can ask by radio or on the Discord server (make sure you're talking with a rank 10+).

    The Army is our brothers. It's fun to work with them and protect our city.

  • Vacation

    You're still a police officer, and you must still follow rules on vacation.

    You cannot have any violations; in self-defense, you must have proof; otherwise, you can get punished.

    You can do civil jobs.

    You cannot enter F0 and F2. If you die, you can spawn a car and exit station, or you can use the elevator to go to F1.

    You cannot take equipment from the Arsenal Room! No weapons, ammo, vests, etc.

    Police Radio is allowed.

  • Events

    You cannot join the Material Theft Event, even if you're on vacation.

    You can join the Harbour Heist Event, but you can't use police cars there.

  • Trainings

    You cannot organize trainings.You cannot organize trainings.

    Trainings are leaded by ranks 10 and over or authorized ranks 7+.

  • Parking

    Do not park vehicles inside these floor 0 garage areas (photo). Keep them free for the pedestrian area and patrols.

    Park your personal vehicles next to the private car spawn.

    Do not park on car spawns.

    Do not block the exit.

  • Special Missions

    You cannot do any raiding or clearing wanted list missions.

    You cannot organize raids or "clearing the wanted list".
