Rules and Info (For low ranks):

  • PD - Police Department

    have 3 floors (f0, f1, f2)

    f0 is garage, arsenal room, hospital..

    f1 is jail room, waiting room for interview, kitchen..

    f2 is mission room, meeting room, offices..

  • Basic RP - RolePlay

    If you die you must wait 15 min, after that you can go back to action.

    Don't use tazer for long distances and don't use it against deadly weapons.

    Drive safety to avoid VDM also named DB (DriveBy). When you cross someone stop, say sorry and make sure he is okay.

    For example, when it's You vs +3 Criminals, try find safe place and call in radio backup, Try not to be part of the fights until you get a higher rank.

  • Green Zone at HP - Hospital

    Inside the hospital you never can't arrest. (inside hospital is safe place to chill out with other players)

    Outside and near hospital is green zone. When you see breaking RP rules, like Car killing (VDM/DB), collect evidence in photo or better in video form and report it to Admins.

    Don't arrest borned criminals outside HP, give them 10 min to breath.

  • Army Base

    You cannot go to Army Base without a good reason and permission by Rank 10+. You can ask by radio or on Discord server (make sure you talking with rank 10+)

    Army is our brothers, it's fun work with Army and protect our city.

  • Vacation

    You're still Police Officer on vacation and you must still follow rules on vacation.

    You cannot have any violations, in self defense you must have proofs otherwise you can get punishment.

    You can do civil jobs.

    You cannot enter f0 and f2. If you die you can spawn car and exit station or you can use elevator to go f1.

    You cannot take equipment from Arenal Room! No weapons, ammo, vests, etc.

    Police Radio is allowed.

  • Events

    You cannot join Material Theft Event, even if you're on vacation.

    You can join Harbour Heist Event, but you can't use police cars there.

  • Trainings

    You cannot organize trainings.

    Trainings is leaded by ranks 10 and over or authorized rank 7+.

  • Parking

    Do not park vehicles inside these floor 0 garage areas (photo). Keep them free for pedestrain area and patrols.

    Park your personal vehicles next to private car spawn.

    Do not park on police car spawns.

    Do not block the exit.

  • Special Missions

    You cannot do any raid or cleaning wanted list missions.

    You cannot organize raids or Clearing Wanted List.
